Material Guide

TransparentMost light transmits through.
TranslucentSome light transmits, but it scatters.
OpaqueLight does not transmit through; energy is absorbed.
ReflectiveLight bounces back in a new direction.

Mixed Materials: Real materials can reflect, absorb, and transmit energy in different amounts.

R = Reflected A = Absorbed T = Transmitted
