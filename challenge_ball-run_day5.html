Communicate and Reflect

Today you complete the challenge. You will tell the engineering story, take a short knowledge check, and submit your final record.

Today's work

Engineers communicate how their designs changed over time. Strong explanations include decisions, evidence, revisions, and trade-offs. The goal is not to pretend the first design was perfect. The goal is to show how testing improved your thinking.

Tell the whole story. Include what worked, what did not, what you changed, and what the evidence showed.

Learning target

I can explain my engineering process and use evidence to evaluate my final design.

By the end of today you should be able to summarize your full week of engineering work and show what you learned.

What You Practiced

A summary of the engineering habits you built.

Think Like an Engineer

A short reminder for future challenges.

Good engineers use evidence and learn from designs that do not work at first. A design that fails is not a failure. It is data.
