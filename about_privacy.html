Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

LyfeLabz is an educational website created to support science learning for middle school students and the broader public. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit the site.

📊 Data

Information We Collect

LyfeLabz does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or student identifiers.

The site uses basic website analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This may include:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages viewed
  • General geographic region (city level)
  • Device type and browser
  • Referring websites

This information is collected in aggregate and does not identify individual users.

🔍 Usage

How Information Is Used

Analytics data is used solely to:

  • Improve website content
  • Understand which resources are most helpful
  • Monitor overall site performance

LyfeLabz does not sell, trade, or share visitor data.

🧒 Children

LyfeLabz is designed for educational use. The site does not require accounts, logins, or submission of personal information. No personal data is knowingly collected from children.
