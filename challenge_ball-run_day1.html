The mission

Slow is the goal

You and your team will design a ball run made of paper and tape. The goal is to keep the ball moving for as long as possible before it reaches the finish. Speed is the trap. A slow, steady run wins.

Criteria and constraints

Criteria are what success looks like. Constraints are the rules you must follow.

Criteria

Read what success looks like for this challenge.

  • The ball travels for as long as possible.
  • The ball reaches the finish without help.
  • Your design works reliably across many trials.
  • The structure stands on its own.

Constraints

Read the rules your team must follow while you build.

  • Build with the approved paper and tape only.
  • Every team starts with the same materials.
  • Do not touch the ball or the structure during a run.
  • A ball stuck for 10 seconds counts as unsuccessful.
