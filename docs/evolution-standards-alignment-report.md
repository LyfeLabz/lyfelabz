## 8. Recommendations for Grade 8 Curriculum Organization

1. **Create a dedicated Grade 8 Evolution unit** in `index.html` (or a future Grade 8 index page) that lists Beetle Island and Evolution Clicker as core resources rather than enrichment links.

2. **Move Beetle Island and Evolution Clicker** from the Grade 6 Biological Evolution unit links to a Grade 8 Natural Selection unit when Grade 8 curriculum pages are built. Currently both appear in the Grade 6 unit at `index.html` lines 1024–1026.

3. **Develop Grade 8–specific reflection questions** for Beetle Island and Evolution Clicker that reference 8.MS-LS4-4 and 8.MS-LS4-5 explicitly (inherited variation, artificial selection).

4. **Consider splitting `lesson_biological-evolution.html`** into two lessons: a Grade 6 lesson focused on fossil evidence and anatomical structures (6.MS-LS4-1, 6.MS-LS4-2), and a Grade 8 lesson focused on natural selection and population change (8.MS-LS4-4, 8.MS-LS4-5). The current single lesson serves both grades but may benefit from explicit grade-level delineation as the curriculum matures.

5. **Add 8.MS-LS4-5 (artificial selection)** considerations to Evolution Clicker or Beetle Island if those resources are expanded — the "environment switching" mechanic in Beetle Island and the unlock system in Evolution Clicker could be extended to model artificial selection scenarios.
